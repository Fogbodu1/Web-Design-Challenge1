Humidity and Latitude






Making a scatter plot to determine the correlation between Latitude and Humidity. While Humidity varies among cities even at the same Latitude, the data points doesn't appear to be closer, and we cannot determine a positive linear relationship between Latitude and Humidity. However, we can conclude that there is variance in humidity that tends to decrease at certain latitudes.
